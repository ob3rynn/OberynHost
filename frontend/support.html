Support

Support for your hosting service.

Support replies within one business day for account, billing, panel-access, and hosting-related issues. Most orders are prepared later the same day or within one business day.

Support covers

  • billing and account questions
  • panel access and login issues
  • hosting-related issues tied to the purchased server
  • questions about order status and fulfillment

Support does not cover

  • support for your players or community members
  • community moderation or in-game disputes
  • ongoing management of your server after delivery

Who should contact support

Support is intended for the server owner. If someone on your server needs help with gameplay, moderation, or your community rules, they should reach out through your server instead.
