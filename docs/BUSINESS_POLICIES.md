# OberynHost Business Policies

Working source of truth for pre-launch business rules, support boundaries, and customer-facing policy decisions.

## 1. Support Promise

- Most orders are prepared later the same day or within one business day.
- Support replies within one business day for account, billing, panel-access, and hosting-related issues.
- Support is provided only to the server owner and does not extend to that server's players or in-game community management.

## 2. Refund And Cancellation Policy

- Orders may be canceled for a full refund any time before fulfillment begins.
- After fulfillment, the original subscription is eligible for a full refund within the first 3 days from the original purchase date.
- After that 3-day window, refunds are not guaranteed and are only issued when required to resolve a billing error, duplicate charge, or service-related problem.
- Refund eligibility for the original purchase should be treated separately from renewal charges.

## 3. Acceptable Use And Abuse Policy

- Customers may not use the service for illegal activity, malicious activity, spam, phishing, malware distribution, denial-of-service activity, unauthorized intrusion, or attempts to disrupt the platform or other users.
- Fraudulent payments, repeated abusive chargebacks, or other patterns of payment abuse may result in suspension or termination.
- Server owners are responsible for setting and enforcing rules within their own communities.
- Communities hosted on the platform may not be centered around hate or extremist activity, sexual exploitation, or harassment-targeted behavior.
- Services may be suspended immediately when necessary to protect infrastructure, other customers, or the platform itself.

## 4. Billing And Renewal Policy

- Subscriptions renew every 30 days.
- If a renewal payment fails, a 7-day grace period begins.
- If payment is not received within that 7-day grace period, the server will be suspended.
- Suspended services may remain recoverable for up to 30 days from suspension, or until payment is received.
- If the subscription remains unpaid for 30 days after suspension, the server and associated user account may be permanently removed.

## 5. Customer Handoff And Delivery Email

The fulfillment email sent from `support@oberynn.com` should be guided rather than credentials-only.

Each fulfillment email should include:

- confirmation that the order is ready
- the panel URL
- the username or email used for login
- a temporary password or password setup instruction
- a short server summary, including server name, server size or tier, and current game or software support
- short first-step instructions for logging in and getting started
- a support boundary note explaining that hosting, billing, account access, and panel issues are supported, while player and community support remain the responsibility of the server owner
- a reply path back to `support@oberynn.com` for hosting-related support

## 6. Chargebacks, Disputes, And "I Paid But Nothing Happened"

- If a customer reports payment without delivery, verify payment and order status first.
- If payment is confirmed and fulfillment has not been completed, make a reasonable effort to resolve the issue before the customer needs to escalate.
- If fulfillment cannot be completed in a reasonable time, or the customer no longer wants to proceed before delivery, issue a refund in line with the refund policy.
- Duplicate charges and billing errors should be corrected promptly.
- A single dispute or payment complaint is not automatically treated as abuse.
- Repeated abusive chargebacks, fraudulent payments, or attempts to retain service after a reversed payment may result in suspension or termination.

## 7. Launch Capacity And Inventory Limits

- Capacity is limited to 22 active server slots.
- Orders are accepted only while inventory is available.
- Once all slots are filled, new purchases stop until a slot becomes available.
- Each server slot is hard-allocated with fixed resources.
- Upgrades or expansions are not available after purchase.

## 8. Included Vs Not Included

- OberynHost fulfills the purchased server as listed and delivers panel access by email.
- After delivery, the server owner is responsible for operating and managing the server.
- Support covers billing, account access, panel access, and hosting-related issues.
- Support does not include player support, community moderation, or ongoing management of the customer's game server.

## 9. Bookkeeping And Records

- Stripe is the source of truth for billing and payment records.
- The app and admin system are the source of truth for fulfillment and service state.
- Manual notes are only needed for exceptions, unusual support cases, refunds outside the normal policy, suspensions, or disputes.

## 10. Incident, Outage, And Delay Response

- When an issue is reported, verify whether it is billing, fulfillment, panel access, or service availability.
- Acknowledge the issue clearly and provide the next expected update when appropriate.
- Fix confirmed access, billing, fulfillment, or service issues as quickly as possible.
- If service cannot be delivered or restored in a reasonable time, offer a refund or other fair resolution consistent with the refund policy.
- If infrastructure protection requires immediate suspension or interruption, platform stability takes priority.
- Customers should be contacted proactively when an outage or delay is extensive or expected to last longer than intended.

## Policy Notes

- These policies are internal source-of-truth rules first and may later be translated into shorter customer-facing policy pages or support copy.
