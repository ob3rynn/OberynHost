What happens next

After your server details are submitted, your order moves into preparation. Your panel link and login will be emailed to you from support@oberynn.com when the server is ready.

When to expect delivery

Most orders are prepared later the same day or within one business day. Orders submitted during weekday business hours are typically prepared after 4:30 PM CT. Orders submitted on Friday evening or over the weekend are typically prepared on the next business day.

Support contact
