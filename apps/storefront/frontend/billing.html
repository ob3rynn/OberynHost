Billing

Billing and renewal terms.

Subscriptions renew every 30 days. Failed renewals receive a 7-day grace period before suspension.

Refunds before and after delivery

  • orders may be canceled for a full refund any time before fulfillment begins
  • the original subscription is eligible for a full refund within the first 3 days from the original purchase date
  • after that 3-day window, refunds are only issued for billing errors, duplicate charges, or service-related problems

Renewals and failed payments

  • subscriptions renew every 30 days
  • failed renewals start a 7-day grace period
  • if payment is not received within that period, the server is suspended
  • suspended service remains recoverable for up to 30 days after suspension, or until payment is received
  • if the subscription remains unpaid after that period, the service becomes eligible for operator-reviewed permanent removal

Server sizing

Each server is sold with fixed resources and cannot be upgraded or expanded after purchase.
