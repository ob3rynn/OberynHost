Guidelines

Baseline rules for hosted communities.

Server owners are responsible for setting and enforcing rules within their own communities. OberynHost still maintains baseline platform rules for what may be hosted.

Not allowed on the platform

  • illegal activity, malicious activity, spam, phishing, malware distribution, or denial-of-service activity
  • unauthorized intrusion or attempts to disrupt the platform or other users
  • communities centered around hate or extremist activity
  • sexual exploitation content
  • harassment-targeted communities or behavior

Community responsibility

You remain responsible for how your server is operated, how your players are managed, and how community rules are enforced inside your own server.
